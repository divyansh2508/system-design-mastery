▸ Show the math

Estimation heuristics: 1 day ≈ 86,400 s (round to ~100,000). Round users/payloads to the nearest power of 10. 1 KB=10³ B, 1 MB=10⁶, 1 GB=10⁹, 1 TB=10¹², 1 PB=10¹⁵. A back-of-envelope answer is meant to be within ~1 order of magnitude — speed and reasoning beat precision.
